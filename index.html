User Guide

How to use FinSage.
Step by step.

01

First Launch & Grant SMS Permission

When you open FinSage for the first time, a permission prompt will appear asking to Read SMS messages. Tap "Allow" ‚Äî this is the only permission FinSage needs. Without it, automatic transaction tracking cannot work.

üí° If you accidentally denied the permission, go to Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí FinSage ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí SMS ‚Üí Allow.
üîí FinSage only reads bank transaction SMS. Your personal messages are never accessed.
02

Auto SMS Tracking

FinSage works silently in the background. Every time your bank sends a debit or credit alert, FinSage:

  • Detects and parses the SMS instantly
  • Extracts merchant, amount, and date
  • Auto-categorizes using 3-layer logic
  • Saves the transaction ‚Äî no action needed
HDFC ICICI SBI Federal Bank + All other banks
03

Import Past Transactions

Already have months of bank SMS on your phone? Import everything at once:

  1. Go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation)
  2. Under "Data & Maintenance", choose your Import Period (3, 6, 12 months, or All time)
  3. Tap "Start Import" and wait ‚Äî progress is shown live
  4. Your full transaction history appears instantly on the Dashboard
‚ö° Import runs in the background ‚Äî you can continue using the app while it works.
04

Navigate the Dashboard

The Dashboard is your financial command center. Here's what each part does:

üìÖ
Month Navigation

Swipe left or right anywhere on the screen to move between months. Tap the month title or the ‚åÑ icon to jump to any specific month from a picker.

üí∞
Balance Card

Shows Income, Expenses, and Net flow for the selected month. The savings rate badge turns green when you're saving more than you spend.

üç©
Spending Donut

An interactive donut chart shows your top 4 expense categories plus "Others". Tap any segment to see the exact amount for that category.

üìã
Recent Activity

All transactions grouped by date. Use the filter chip to switch between "This Month", "Last Month", or "All Time". Tap any transaction for full details.

üîÑ
Refresh & Jump to Today

The Refresh icon re-scans SMS for any missed transactions. The Today icon (calendar) takes you straight back to the current month.

üîî
Quick Categorize Notifications

When a new SMS is detected, you may receive a notification that lets you instantly correct the category without ever opening the app.

05

Add Transactions Manually

For cash payments, UPI transfers without SMS, or any other expense not detected automatically:

  1. Tap the + button in the bottom navigation bar
  2. Enter the amount (‚Çπ symbol provided)
  3. Select Expense or Income
  4. Pick a category ‚Äî AI may suggest one automatically
  5. Choose the date using the calendar picker
  6. Optionally add a merchant name and narration
  7. Tap "Save Transaction"
ü§ñ When you type a merchant name, the AI may automatically suggest the right category using a "Use suggested" chip.
06

Read Your Insights

Tap the Insights tab to see a deep analysis of your finances:

  • Financial Overview ‚Äî Income, expenses, savings rate & income/expense ratio for the current month
  • Next Month Forecast ‚Äî AI prediction based on your 3-month spending average
  • Weekly Spending Graph ‚Äî Smooth line chart showing day-by-day spend across the week
  • Spending Trends ‚Äî 6-month rolling bar chart to spot seasonal patterns
  • Top Categories ‚Äî Animated progress bars ranking your biggest expense categories
  • AI Insights Card ‚Äî Gemini-generated summary + actionable suggestions (requires API key in Settings)
07

Create Smart Rules (Auto-Categorization)

Teach FinSage once and it remembers forever. Smart Rules let you map any merchant keyword to a category automatically ‚Äî no AI needed, instant and offline.

‚ûï
Create a Rule

Go to Settings ‚Üí Smart Rules ‚Üí tap the + FAB. Enter a merchant keyword (e.g. "Swiggy") and choose the category (e.g. "Food"). Done.

üîÅ
Apply to Past Transactions

After creating rules, tap "Recategorize" to retroactively apply all rules to your entire transaction history with a live progress bar.

üìä
Rule Analytics

Each rule shows how many transactions it matched and when it last fired ‚Äî so you can see which rules are working hardest for you.

‚ö° Rules are checked before keywords & AI ‚Äî so they're instant, offline, and always take priority.
08

Enable Gemini AI

Unlock AI-powered categorization fallback and weekly insight summaries:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Intelligence & AI
  2. Toggle "Enable Gemini AI" on
  3. Paste your Google AI Studio API Key (free tier available)
  4. Tap "Save API Key"
  5. Optionally enable "Automatic AI Insights" and pick a frequency (6h / 12h / 24h / 48h)
  6. Tap "Generate Insights Now" for an immediate analysis
üîë Get a free API key at aistudio.google.com. The free tier is more than enough for personal use.
üîí Only the merchant name + amount is sent to Gemini ‚Äî never your full SMS or personal info.

Pro Tips & Tricks

  • Long press a transaction on the dashboard for quick actions.
  • Use the filter chip on Recent Activity to switch between time ranges.
  • The Summary Card in Settings lets you customize which stats appear at the top of the transaction list.
  • Add a home screen widget (Financial Pulse, Monthly Spending, or Recent Activity) for at-a-glance stats without opening the app.
  • A savings rate above 20% lights up as green ‚Äî aim for it!
  • The I/E ratio shows "Healthy" when income ‚â• expenses. Anything below 1.0 means you're spending more than you earn.
